Provide a hypothetical FX rate between two currencies to see profitable cross-currency single-hop trades (buy in one currency, sell in the other).

How It Works

  • Enter Currency A and Currency B (case-sensitive match to server currencies).
  • Enter a rate as A : B (e.g. 1 : 1 means 1 A equals 1 B).
  • The tool finds items you can buy in A stores and sell in B stores profitably (and the reverse B -> A).
  • Quantities are limited by stock, demand, and buyer balance.
